President Erdoğan received Ömer Koç and Ali Koç
President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan received Koç Holding Chairman of the Board, Ömer Mehmet Koç and Koç Holding Deputy Chairman of the Board and Ford Otosan Chairman of the Board, Ali Koç.
Having taken place closed to the press in the Presidential Complex, Erdoğan's reception took 40 minutes.
