Measures against FETO in Macedonia
Macedonian Minister of State, Furkan Çako, stated that they have taken steps to find out FETO's extensions also in legal terms within their country and have started the necessary investigations.
Noting that the organization also has activities in the media and business world in Macedonia at the same time, the Minister Çako said, "It is known to the public."
Turks here do not send their children to FETO schools anymore.
We are making an appeal to our people to not send their children to these places.
He added, "Benefiting from the hospitality and favor of the people in the past, this structure is very well known to the public."
