2 year stop for the ferry from Kabataş
The Kabataş ferry dock was closed to sea traffic for two years due to the "Construction Works for Kabataş Square Reorganization and Renovation of the Transfer Hub and Docks" which is being conducted by Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality (IMM).
At Kabataş shore, a modern transfer hub will be constructed to integrate sea, rail and road transportation systems.
An uninterrupted pedestrian flow will be enabled along the shore by bringing the traffic underground.
Tram and funicular systems will continue to operate for a while more.
Round trips will be started to ensure the transportation of people to the Taksim, Eminönü, and Beşiktaş areas.
