Two cyclists hospitalized after collision in Sigulda.
Two cyclists have collided in Sveices Street, Sigulda, suffering various injuries, the State Police reports.
Two men, born in 1987 and 1990, were involved in the accident and both of them were brought to a medical institution.
Another cyclist has suffered injuries after falling from his bicycle in Koknese.
A man born in 1989 fell in 1905 gada Street is being medically treated.
Meanwhile, in Dobele municpality's Naudite parish, a motorcycle driver born in 1989 tried to avoid a collision with a dog, which suddenly ran out on the road, and fell over.
He was also hospitalized.
71 traffic accidents in which 16 persons were injured have happened in Latvia during the last 24 hours.
