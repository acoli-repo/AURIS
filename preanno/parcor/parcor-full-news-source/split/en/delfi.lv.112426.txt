Simple minced pike croquette with white bread.
Consume immediately after done, while the flavors last!
Process pike fillet meat and white bread in the meat grinder.
Add in milk, salt and pepper and process through the grinder or mix through one more time.
Add in butter at room temperature and again mix up well; then make mid-sized croquette pieces.
Roll them around in breadcrumbs.
Heat oil in a frying-pan.
Place the croquettes in the frying-pan and fry for 10 to 15 minutes until golden brown from both sides.
Serve warm and pour over with melted butter or a sauce to your liking.
