Judo fighter Nikiforenko loses in the first fight of the Olympics.
This was a fight in the first round, and the Latvian judo fighter has already concluded his participation in the Olympic Games with the 17th place.
His coach Olegs Baskins admitted to the Latvian Television that Nikiforenko had little hope and it was clear before the contest.
He also revealed that the athlete got a shoulder injury during the fight.
It must be noted that, in order to get his Olympic opportunity, Nikiforenko had to fight in a different weight division than his usual, namely in the weight division of up to 100 kilograms.
The Azerbaijani athlete was 25 kg heavier.
As reported, in the weight division up to 100 kilograms, Jevgenijs Borodavko manged to hold out against Ryunosuke for two and a half minutes, until the opponent implemented a clear victory throw.
Therefore the judo contest in Rio is over for Latvia.
