Veteran Iranian Foreign Affairs Minister is in Parliament!
Çavuşoğlu came to TBMM with his counterpart Zarif.
Firstly, Çavuşoğlu and Zarif observed the damage from the Hall of Honor, then they went up to the floor of the Prime Minister's Office and observed the damage.
Zarif said the following after his visit:
Turkish people taking to the streets proved once more the fact that Turkish people and popular sovereignty surpass all powers.
The Islamic Republic of Iran condemned the coup attempt in the first hour of the coup attempt and in the same way, it stood with the Turkish people and government, and this has always been the case.
My visit to TBMM today is a symbol of the fact that we stand with the Turkish people and we stand with the Turkish democracy.
