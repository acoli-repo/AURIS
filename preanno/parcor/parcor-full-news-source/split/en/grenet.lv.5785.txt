Customer flees with motorcycle during test drive.
On August 10 at 10 AM an unregistered motorcycle Yamaha YZ250 was stolen from a court-yard in the Rembate county, the State Police informs.
The motorcycle's owner had placed an advertisement that he was selling a motorcycle.
A potential buyer arrived at the agreed time, sat on the motorbike supposedly to test-drive it and then rode away.
The staff of Ogre Police of the Riga Region Department of the State Police requests everyone who could provide any information pertaining to the criminal act, to come forward.
Contact: 65002403 (working hours) or 65002400 (24/7).
Information providers will remain anonymous.
