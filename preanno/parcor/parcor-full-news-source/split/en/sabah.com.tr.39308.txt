Muharrem Usta: Match-fixing is different
Trabzonspor President Muharrem Usta offered a friendly hand to Fenerbahçe.
Explaining that Turkey's issue is the issue of all the clubs, Usta said, "We are on the side of this country with 79 million."
We should maintain a strong stance.
If there is a problem of Fenerbahçe with FETO, if an injustice is done to someone, I will also stand behind Fenerbahçe.
He said, "However, the matter of match-fixing between Trabzonspor and Fenerbahçe is different."
