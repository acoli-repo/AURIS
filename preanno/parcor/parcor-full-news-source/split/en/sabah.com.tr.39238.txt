Adil Öksüz's father in law spoke
Indicated as the number 1 in the July 15 coup attempt, FETO member Adil Öksüz's father-in-law and mother-in-law were detained and brought to the court.
Father-in-law Cevat Yıldırım and mother-in-law Hatice Yıldırım were released by the court on the condition of judicial control and an international travel ban.
Speaking after leaving the court house, Cevat Yıldırım made an appeal to his son-in-law to surrender and said the following: "I stand with the state."
God damn the coup plotters.
In fact, I don't even recognize Adil Öksüz, whom I have know for 18 years.
I do not have a son-in-law named Adil Öksüz.
I want him to be arrested immediately.
In this way, he can neither live in Turkey nor anywhere in the world.
Let him come and trust Turkish justice.
If he made a mistake, he will serve his sentence.
He upset us by escaping.
The police and the state are in a hard situation.
The police department is not dealing with anything else for 20-25 days.
Stating that he does not want Turkey to become Syria, Yıldırım said, "We do not need a coup, we need work."
We do not want Turkey to become like Syria.
Syria had a neighbor like Turkey.
It has a president like Recep Tayyip Erdoğan.
He said, "What does the coup have to do with me?"
On the other hand, Öksüz, last seen passing through the TEM Highway Akyazı ticket offices at 03.00 o'clock on July 19, vanished after leaving his car with 'SIR' in the garage of his father-in-law's house.
