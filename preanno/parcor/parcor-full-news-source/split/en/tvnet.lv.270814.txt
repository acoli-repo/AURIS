Drunk and armed man detained in Aspazijas Boulevard.
On Friday, the State Police detained a drunk and armed man in Aspazijas Boulevard, SP representative Gita Gzibovska told.
SP was informed that an apparently drunk man is walking irresponsibly along Aspazijas Boulevard, armed with a handgun.
The police vehicle that responded to the call got involved in a traffic accident and the man was then detained by another police unit.
A gas pistol was found with the intoxicated man.
As reported, a State Police (SP) car got involved in a car accident in Riga center on Friday when it was rushing to respond to a call.
The accident occurred when it responded to category A call, namely it was heading to prevent a situation in which health or life is under threat.
Unfortunately it collided with another car at the intersection of Krisjana Barona Street and Dzirnavu Street.
