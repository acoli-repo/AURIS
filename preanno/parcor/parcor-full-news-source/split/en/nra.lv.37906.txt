Car accident in Riga center involves a police car.
A car accident involving a police car occurred in Riga center this afternoon.
The police car with turned on sound and light signals was in a hurry for a top level call when it collided with another car at the intersection of Krisjana Barona Street and Dzirnavu Street.
The police is investigating the conditions, but the initial information reveals that the second car did not give way to the police car.
A woman has suffered in the accident.
