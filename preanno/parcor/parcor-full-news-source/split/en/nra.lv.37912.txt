New playgrounds in Riga for children and happier parents.
This summer the Riga City Council has introduced two more playgrounds, in Kipsala and Ciekurkalns, which are already available for children.
A spacious, multifunctional ship-like play-structure with various climbing elements, little bridges, games, tubes and slides has been set up on the Kipsala beach.
The playground is intended for children of all ages.
There are also swings and various balancing challenges.
The nautically-themed fantasy world is enhanced by its location near the Daugava riverbank, opening a view to the Passenger Terminal and cruise ships.
Another playground, erected between Ciekurkalns Avenues No. 4 and No. 5, contains a play house, trampoline, step track, balance bridge, swings, play balls and other play elements as well as an exercise zone with special rings for strength and coordination training, parallel bars and other devices.
The playground's floor is covered with a colorful rubber mat.
A new, cheerfully colored pavement connects the playground to the street.
