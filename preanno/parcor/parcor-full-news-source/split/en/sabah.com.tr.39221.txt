Kazakh Balandin made history
One of the biggest surprises in swimming history happened with the victory of Kazakh Dmitriy Balandin yesterday morning.
Balandin obtained victory in 2 minutes, 7 seconds and 46 split seconds in the 200 meter butterfly stroke in which USA swimmers were seen as favorites.
American John Prenot, with his score of 2:07.53, and Russian, Chupkov with his score of 2:07.70, followed Balandin who won the first medal in swimming in Kazakh history by taking gold.
