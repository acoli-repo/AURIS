Rain and colder weather expected in some locations.
On Friday, in Latvia one can expect intermittent rain and a temperature drop by approximately one degree Celsius, LETA learned from the Environment, Geology and Meteorology Center of Latvia.
There might be thunder at some locations.
According to forecasts, it is going to rain mainly in Kurzeme and northern Vidzeme.
The maximum air temperature will not exceed 16 to 19 degrees Celsius.
During the day, there will be no precipitation and mostly sunny in Riga.
The air temperature will not rise above 18 degrees Celsius.
