Kalvins: Meldonium could be excluded from the Prohibited Substances List for athletes.
It is expected that Meldonium contained in Mildronats, a medical drug manufactured in Latvia, could be removed from the Prohibited Substances List for athletes within this year, Ivars Kalvins, the Director of Latvian Organic Synthesis Institute, revealed on LNT's morning news show 900 sekundes on Friday.
He said that he is going to Canada in September to discuss the Meldonium issue with the World Anti-Doping Agency's (WADA) officials.
The scientist expressed his hope that Mildronats would be removed from the blacklist already this year.
