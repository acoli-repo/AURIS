Zbignevs Stankevics: when the time comes, the truth is resurrected.
The archbishop of the Latvian Catholic Church, Zbignevs Stankevics in an interview with Neatkariga emphasizes that if we want to obtain energy in our life, the word of God must be in its center.
It is because the power of truth is greater than evil's whim.
Many have started on their way to the Assumption Day celebration (15 August);
What message will be received by the followers of religion this year?
In the recent years, the Pope's nuncio has expressed a desire to lead the main church service at Aglona.
Therefore, the content of the main message is left at his discretion.
Bishop Janis Bulis leading the Procession of the Cross is another tradition, which has been already accepted.
He will also bring out his message.
I lead the service of vigilia on the day before.
Last year, I addressed Pope Francis' encyclical "Laudato si" and rose the topic of "common home" and the subject of Earth in the context of Latvia.
This year... Of course, I follow the world events and try to address them in my speech.
My approach conforms to the church opinion, namely, that the Gospel message must be at the center of one's life.
It must be shown that the Gospel responds to the most painful problems of person's life and inspires them in their further path.
In that way, it speaks to people.
But in Cesis, at the Lampa discussion event you said that "we have to return to an understanding of the complete man".
What did you mean by that?
Does it mean that a person turns away from the message spread by the church?
Does the church have something to offer to facilitate such return today?
