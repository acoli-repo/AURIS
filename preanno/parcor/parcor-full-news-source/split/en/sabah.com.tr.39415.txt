"Çiçek" crisis in the Eagles - Sports News
It was reported that Şeref Çiçek, one of the assistant trainers of Technical director, Şenol Güneş, did not participate in training yesterday.
It was reported that Çiçek did not like his wage increase and for this reason, did not participate in training to show his reaction in the case that has reverted to the management.
It was stated that the management is waiting to hear from Şenol Güneş before responding to the case.
