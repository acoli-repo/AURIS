They converted the coach into a bridal car
The couple, during their wedding ceremony, drew great attention by making a convoy different to conventional wedding convoys.
Making a convoy with carts, the couple preferred a coach as the bridal car.
Creating the convoy with around 10 carts following the instrumentalist group, the couple received great attention from bystanders.
Touring around Keşan city center, the convoy then went to the wedding house.
