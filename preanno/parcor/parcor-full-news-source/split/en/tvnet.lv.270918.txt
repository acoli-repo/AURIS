Kurdish activists occupy German public broadcaster's building.
A group consisting of 30 people yesterday took over the public broadcaster WDR building in Dusseldorf where they held an almost two hour long protest, the German Police reports.
The demonstrators, who arrived with posters, shouted out various slogans trying to draw attention to the current situation in Turkey.
Officials have reported that the demonstration was more or less peaceful and eventually the protesters left the building voluntarily at employees' request.
