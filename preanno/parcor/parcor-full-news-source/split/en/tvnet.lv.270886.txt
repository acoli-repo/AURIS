Large-scale traffic restrictions come in effect near Aglona.
In order to ensure the safety of participants of the Assumption of Mary celebration, as of August 12 at 5 PM until August 15 at 6 PM, truck transit along several national roads around Aglona will be closed, the SJSC Latvian Sate Roads informed.
These restrictions do not apply to Aglona celebration participants and public transportation.
It must be noted that motor road P62 Kraslava-Preili-Madona in its section from Kraslava to Kraslava railway station (0.80th- 4.00th km) is under reconstruction and only public transport and pedestrians will be allowed to cross the construction area during the celebration in Aglona.
Other traffic participants can use a bypass road, as in the diagram shown in the attached file.
Traditionally this celebration is attended by large numbers of people, including pilgrims.
Therefore, drivers must be particularly careful as there will be intense pedestrian movement along various motor roads toward Aglona.
We request drivers to be tolerant to the traffic restrictions, to comply with traffic organisation rules and to be prepared for a longer travel time.
