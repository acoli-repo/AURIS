11 people were detained in Konya!
Work has started to arrest the people, about whom the decision of detention was given within the scope of the investigation.
Within this scope, 4 officers in Ereğli Municipality and 1 teacher and 6 employees in the Private Merve Şahika School were detained.
Having undergone a medical examination in the State Hospital of Ereğli, the suspects were brought to the Police Department of Ereğli.
